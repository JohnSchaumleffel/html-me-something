About Me

My name is John Schaumleffel. I am 30 years old and currently work as a mechanic. I am married and have a daughter named Sophia. I enjoy all sorts of activities but I am going to focus on the the three that I do the most. Games, golf, and fixing things.

Games

My favorite game to play currently is Dungeons and Dragons. It is a table top role playing game where you assume the identity of some sort of hero, my character is a rogue, and you go on quests with frineds. I have only been playing for about a month but I really enjoy it. It is the most fun you can have while sitting at a table.

Golf

I just recently started to play golf. What began as a one off for a friends bachelor party has turned into a bit of an obsession. I try to go the range at least once a week to practice and weather permitting I try to play at least 9 holes of actual golf once a month.

Fixing Things

I have always liked working with my hands. Whether it be on a vehicle, computer or a piece of furniture. I currently in the process of drawing up plans for a large table that my friends and I can play D&D on. I would also like to try my hand at blacksmithing but my garage is a little too messy to install a forge right now.

Closing

I hope you have enjoyed reading about what I like to do when I am not working buried up to my eyes in prep-work.
